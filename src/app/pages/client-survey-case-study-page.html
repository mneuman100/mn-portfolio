Background

At Origami Risk, I designed two client surveys to understand how clients were using the Help Center and whether the Release Notes format was meeting their needs, before the documentation team made changes to either. Survey distribution and interview coordination were handled by our Product Marketing Manager, allowing me to focus on survey design, interview execution, and synthesis.

Method

Both surveys used skip logic to route participants based on their experience level, producing quantitative response data and open-text feedback across the participants with a combined survey completion rate of 75.2%. To explore themes more deeply, I led five client discovery interviews with volunteer survey participants.

Findings

Most clients were unaware the Help Center existed, and those who used it identified significant content gaps; clients valued the Release Notes but wanted more context about what changed and why.

Results

Research findings directly informed the July 2022 Release Notes format redesign and established the priority order for future documentation content sprints. One client volunteered to serve as Beta Testers as a direct result of the engagement.
