About me

Designing with empathy,
building with purpose

I love digging into a problem to find all the possibilities, as well as learning about people's experiences and what drives their decisions. That curiosity doesn't stay in the research. My mind sometimes runs ahead of the conversation, so if you ask me a question and I take a beat too long to answer, I'm probably already three steps down the line thinking about what gets affected by each possible path before I give you my answer.

Let's get closer

I enjoy learning about other cultures and frequently watch shows from different countries. Let's just say, subtitles were a game-changer. I also love listening to audiobooks, going for hikes, and lately I started my journey in watercolor. I typically mix my PC work with hands-on activities like gardening, puzzles, embroidery, and drawing. I have two crazy cats and love watching the birds that frequent my bird feeder.
