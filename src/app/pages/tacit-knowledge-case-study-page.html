The Challenge

A retiring employee held exclusive knowledge of a monthly Agreement Invoicing process spanning 11 systems with no automatic integration between them. Processes like this — built up from years of workarounds, exceptions, and in-the-moment judgment calls — don't survive a standard knowledge transfer interview. The only way to capture them is to watch them happen.

The Approach

I used contextual inquiry and shadowing sessions, observing the process live and asking follow-up questions in the moment. This surfaced what official documentation never captures: the unofficial steps, edge-case decisions, and hard-won workarounds that only become visible when you watch the work being done.

The Deliverable

Outcomes

  • The resulting workflow map captured tacit knowledge that would have been permanently lost at the employee's departure.
  • The documentation served as a self-sufficient onboarding reference — the incoming employee learned the full process independently, without any subject matter expert assistance.
