Background

At ProviDyn, I was asked to document a retiring employee's complex Agreement Invoicing process before her departure. The monthly workflow spanned 11 systems with no automatic integration between them.

Method

Using contextual inquiry and shadowing sessions with follow-up questions in the moment, I documented the process as it was actually performed. I captured not just the official steps but the workarounds, exceptions, and judgment calls that only become visible by watching her work.

Results

The resulting workflow map captured tacit knowledge that would have been lost when she departed. The outcome was a self-sufficient onboarding reference that allowed the next person to learn the process independently, without SME assistance.
